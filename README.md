# Nabdh Al-Taaleem, Women in Saudi ICT

A data visualization project analyzing the participation of Saudi women in the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) sector, benchmarked against Vision 2030 targets and global standards.

## Live Site
https://raghadfraihanalharthi-tech.github.io/nabdh-altaaleem/

## Overview
This project transforms official Saudi government open data into an interactive analytical dashboard. It tracks the growth of women's participation in ICT from 2020 to 2024, compares it against international benchmarks, and visualizes the gap remaining before Vision 2030 targets are met.

## Data Sources
All data points are fully cited and traceable to their original sources.

Saudi Open Data Platform (open.data.gov.sa) — Women's participation rate in ICT sector 2020 to 2024

Saudi Open Data Platform (open.data.gov.sa) — Male and female workforce ratio in ICT 2024

Vision 2030 Official Document (vision2030.gov.sa) — Women's labor market participation target

International Telecommunication Union ITU 2024 — Global share of women in technology roles

## Key Findings
Women's participation in Saudi ICT grew from 24.08% in 2020 to 34.50% in 2024, surpassing both the Vision 2030 target of 30% and the global benchmark. Despite this progress, women represent only 10% of the total ICT workforce, indicating that while representation rates are improving, absolute workforce numbers remain a significant challenge.
